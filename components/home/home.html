Who I am.

Originally from Washington, D.C. I grew up on the east coast and after 18 years I decided that I wanted a change of scenery (my mom still isn't happy about it, sorry Mom!). Called by the outdoors, a sense of adventure, and higher education, I moved to Salt Lake City, UT, the place I now proudly call home.

I received a B.S. in Criminal Justice from Westminster College, but shortly before graduating I realized that my true passion lies in coding. I joined the workforce, but struggled to find job satisfaction, so I turned to programming and can happily say that today I am a full-stack web developer.
